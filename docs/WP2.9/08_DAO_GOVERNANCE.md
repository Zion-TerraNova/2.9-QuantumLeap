ZION implements a **gradual decentralization model** â€” a carefully planned 20-year transition from founder leadership to full community control. This approach balances the need for decisive early-stage development with the ultimate goal of true decentralization.

### Why Gradual Transition?

| Instant Decentralization | ZION's Gradual Approach |
|--------------------------|-------------------------|
| Governance chaos (Bitcoin forks) | Stability with clear roadmap |
| Contentious debates (Ethereum splits) | Structured decision-making |
| Founder abandonment risk | Committed 20-year presence |
| Community unprepared | Community matures with responsibility |
| No accountability | Clear milestones and checkpoints |
